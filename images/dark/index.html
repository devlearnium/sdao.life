SDAO Mission.

In a broader context the mission of SDAO is to transform the human environment, the system of relationships, to find a harmonious and safe home, to organize a sustainable and ecological cohabitation of a group of people connected by common values in one territory.

The mission of Sustainable CAO is to build a Sustainable Crypto-City by private investors and initiatives, demonstrating the whole process of project implementation in order to share knowledge in this field. The energy of the CCG (Sustainable Crypto-City) is derived exclusively from RES (Renewable Energy Sources).

The city is managed by the DAO. The internal currency is the SDAO Token, which is backed by UCG mining. UKG uses heat from mining as a means of heating homes as well as heating for agriculture and other technological projects, using hot air or water (wood drying, winemaking, brewing). The UKG provides its connection to the Internet by means of global satellite Internet coverage, such as Star Link.

Each SDAO founder, after the actual creation of an SDAO token, will be credited with a proportionate number of SDAO tokens. Once the minimum amount is collected on the SDAO temporary wallet, the Administrator will initiate the creation of the SDAO and all necessary infrastructure.
