SDAO Mission

Transformation of the human environment

In a broader context, the mission of SDAO is the transformation of the human environment, the system of relationships, the acquisition of a harmonious and safe home, the organization of sustainable and environmentally friendly cohabitation of a group of people connected by common values in the same territory.

The mission of the Sustainable DAO is to build a Sustainable Crypto City (SCC) through private investors and initiatives, showcasing the entire project implementation process to share knowledge in this area. The energy of the SCC is obtained exclusively from RES (Renewable Energy Sources). City management is carried out within the DAO.

The internal currency is the SDAO Token, the internal security of which is supported by the mining of the SCC. SCC uses the heat from mining as a means of heating houses, as well as heating for agriculture and other technological projects, using hot air or water (wood drying, winemaking, brewing). SCC provides its connection to the Internet using global satellite Internet coverage, such as Star Link. Budget planning and decision-making within the SCC is carried out by voting on a proportional system, where the strength of the vote depends on the number of tokens in the wallet.

What is DAO?

DAO is a decentralized autonomous organization. Well, the most obvious thing is that DAOs work on the blockchain.

What makes DAO different from other companies or communities?

From the organizations we are used to, DAO is distinguished by a horizontal management structure, collective decision-making, transparency in the movement of funds, and geographical distribution of participants.

Who makes the main decisions in the DAO?

“Think of the DAO,” urges the official Ethereum website, “as a native internet business that is collectively owned and operated by its members. This business has a treasury that no one has access to without group approval. Decisions are made through proposals and votes…. There is no CEO who can authorize spending based on his own whims. And there is no chance that the dodgy CFO will fix something in the ledgers. Everything is open and the rules regarding spending are built into the DAO through their code.”

How is the voting process going?

The members of this organizational structure are equally responsible for its progress, since all key decisions are taken by a general vote. At the same time, everyone can choose the amount of time and effort that he is willing to invest in order for this progress to be achieved. The more effort you put in, the more tokens you hold, which means you have more voting power.

Can I put questions to a vote?

Yes, each member of the DAO can put questions to the vote. DAO is a form of organization that provides an opportunity to build a self-governing community with common interests, in which everyone feels the value of their contribution.

Why are DAOs becoming popular?

Of course, one can refer to the novelty effect, but it would be fundamentally wrong to reduce the emerging popularity of the DAO to the usual network hype. One of the main reasons why more entrepreneurs, artists, and activists are getting involved in the DAO is that these structures are changing the way organizations and systems should be run.
